## Overview

You can link Ozon, Wildberries, and other marketplaces right from the SellerClub dashboard in a few steps.

## Steps

1. Navigate to **Integrations** in SellerClub.
2. Click **Add Marketplace**.
3. Choose your platform (e.g., Ozon, Wildberries).
4. Enter your marketplace account credentials or API key.
5. Click **Connect**.
6. Wait for confirmation. You'll see the marketplace listed if successful.

Your marketplace is now synced with MoySklad via SellerClub.
