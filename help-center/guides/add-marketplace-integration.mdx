---

## Overview

Set up a new marketplace connection in SellerClub to automate sync with MoySklad.

## Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    In the SellerClub dashboard, click on **Integrations** from the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Add Marketplace">
    Click the **Add Marketplace** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Marketplace">
    Pick Ozon, Wildberries, or another supported partner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Enter your marketplace account's credentials or paste the API key/token as prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish & Test">
    Click **Connect**. Once connected, use the **Test Sync** button to verify everything is set up.
  </Step>
</Steps>
