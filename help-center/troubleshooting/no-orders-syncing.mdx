---
title: Orders Not Syncing from Marketplace
description: Fix when orders from Ozon/Wildberries do not appear in MoySklad via SellerClub.
---

## Symptoms

- New orders from marketplace are missing in MoySklad
- No update in SellerClub order log

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Integration Status">
    Go to **Integrations > Marketplaces** and confirm status is "Connected" for the affected marketplace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect if Needed">
    Click **Reconnect** and re-enter API credentials. Confirm successful connection message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resync Orders">
    In SellerClub, use the **Sync Now** option under the marketplace integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
